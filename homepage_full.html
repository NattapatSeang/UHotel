Tucked away on a quiet street next to Bangkok's famous Sukhumvit 11 nightlife scene,
the Solitaire Hotel offers you a relaxed ambiance in a central location. Just minutes
away from famous hospitals, nightlife and the Skytrain, you can easily reach some of
the city's most popular destinations in minutes.

What's more, if you are searching for a 4 star hotel located in the heart of Bangkok,
Solitaire Hotel is a perfect choice.
